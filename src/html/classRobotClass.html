char  connectionTypeIn 
)

Constructor with two arguments, reactionTypeIn and connectionTypeIn.

This constructor takes in two character arguments. The first, reactionTypeIn, sets the robot reactionType as either 'E' (excitatory) or 'I' (inhibitory). The second, connectionTypeIn, sets the robot connectionType as either 'D' (direct) or 'C' (crossed). The other arguments, x and y, determine its location

char RobotClass::getConnectionType ( )

Returns the robot's connectionType.

Returns the robot's connectionType: 'D' (direct) or 'C' (crossed). The former type of robot will have left wheel speed controlled by the left sensor and right wheel speed by the right sensor; the latter type will have left wheel speed controlled by the right sensor, and vice versa.

char connectionType
